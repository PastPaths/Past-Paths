At a glance

We only ask for what we need to respond properly.

Most contact with Past Paths begins by email. Any details you share are used to understand your enquiry and reply to you.

Information you may share with us

You may provide your name, email address, organisation, site or project details, and any other information you choose to include when contacting Past Paths.

How we use it

We use this information to respond to enquiries, discuss possible projects, prepare relevant follow-up information, and manage normal business communication.

How we store it

Messages and project correspondence may be retained for business records, continuity and follow-up. We do not sell personal information.

Third-party services

The website may rely on standard hosting, email and analytics services. These services may process limited technical information needed to operate the website and deliver messages.

Your choices

You can ask us to update or remove information you have shared with us where it is reasonable and lawful to do so.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@pastpaths.co.uk.
