About Past Paths

Digital history experiences built on research, place and partnership.

Past Paths creates carefully researched digital audio experiences for heritage organisations. Each experience is designed to help visitors understand a site at their own pace, while supporting the organisations that care for historic places.

What We Do

We turn historic places into self-guided digital experiences.

Past Paths works with heritage organisations to create researched, narrated audio experiences that visitors can access through the Past Paths app.

The aim is simple: to make high-quality interpretation more accessible, without requiring sites to take on the cost, staffing or technical work of building their own digital platform.

Principles

Every experience is shaped by three principles.

01

Research-led

Each experience begins with historical, architectural and archaeological research, so the finished tour feels grounded and trustworthy.

02

Visitor-focused

Stories are written for people exploring a real place, with clear pacing, accessible language and a strong sense of atmosphere.

03

Partner-minded

Experiences are created to complement existing interpretation and support the long-term needs of heritage organisations.

Background

A practical way to bring more history to more visitors.

Past Paths was created to give heritage sites a flexible way to share deeper interpretation with visitors, while keeping the experience simple to access and easy to maintain.

By combining careful research, engaging writing and app-based delivery, Past Paths gives historic places another way to tell their stories beyond panels, leaflets and scheduled guided tours.
