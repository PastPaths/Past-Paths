About Past Paths

Research-led audio interpretation for historic places.

Past Paths creates digital audio experiences for heritage organisations, helping visitors understand historic places through carefully researched, accessible storytelling.

Partner with us See our process →

What we do

We turn research into visitor experiences people can follow at their own pace.

Each Past Paths experience combines historical research, architectural observation and clear interpretation, designed to support heritage partners without adding pressure to staff, volunteers or existing budgets.

01

Research first

Every script is built around evidence, context and the specific character of the site.

02

Visitor focused

Audio experiences are written to be easy to follow on site, with a clear route through the story.

03

Partner led

We work with the organisations who know their places best, so interpretation feels accurate and appropriate.

Our purpose

Making local history easier to access.

Past Paths is designed for historic sites, trusts, churches, councils and community heritage projects that want high-quality interpretation without needing to build their own app or audio platform.

01

Accuracy

Interpretation should be engaging, but it should also be trustworthy.

02

Atmosphere

The best audio experiences help visitors notice the place around them.

03

Access

Digital interpretation should support independent visitors and different learning styles.

04

Partnership

We create experiences with heritage organisations, not simply for them.
