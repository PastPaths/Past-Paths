What is Past Paths?

Past Paths creates professionally produced digital audio experiences for heritage organisations across the United Kingdom. Each experience is tailored to the individual site, helping visitors explore at their own pace while supporting the organisation through shared revenue.

No upfront costWe cover production costs.
Revenue sharedPartners receive a share of each sale.
Bespoke contentEvery tour is written for the site.

How It Works

For Heritage Partners

For Visitors

Past Paths helps visitors discover the stories behind historic places through carefully written and narrated audio experiences. Each tour is designed to be simple, accessible and enjoyable on site.

Our experiences are created for castles, churches, archaeological sites, museums, historic houses and heritage landscapes.
