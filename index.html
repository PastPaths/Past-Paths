About

Past Paths

Past Paths creates carefully researched digital audio experiences for heritage organisations. Combining architectural and historical expertise with engaging interpretation; our experiences allow visitors to explore each site at their own pace.

Process

Our Process

  1. Discovery

    We begin by understanding the organisation, the site and its visitors.

  2. Research

    Historical and architectural research forms the foundation of every experience.

  3. Historical Interpretation

    Research is developed into an engaging narrative that remains faithful to the evidence.

  4. Production

    The experience is written, narrated, edited and prepared for delivery through Past Paths.

  5. Launch

    The completed experience becomes available through the Past Paths app.

  6. Partnership

    Revenue is shared and the experience can continue to develop over time.
