At a glance

We only ask for what we need to respond properly.

Most contact with Past Paths begins by email. Any details you share are used to understand your enquiry, reply clearly and manage normal project communication.

Information you may share with us

You may provide your name, email address, organisation, role, site or project details, and any other information you choose to include when contacting Past Paths.

How we use that information

We use the information you provide to respond to enquiries, understand potential projects, prepare relevant follow-up information, arrange conversations and manage normal business communication.

Project correspondence

If an enquiry develops into a project discussion, we may keep related messages, notes and documents so that we can maintain continuity, record decisions and refer back to agreed details.

How we store it

Correspondence may be stored in standard email, document and business administration systems. We take reasonable steps to keep information secure and do not sell personal information.

Third-party services

This website and our communication systems may rely on standard hosting, email, analytics or file-management services. These services may process limited technical or contact information needed to operate the website, deliver messages and support normal business activity.

Website data

Like most websites, limited technical information may be processed when you visit, such as device, browser or usage information. This helps the website function correctly and gives a basic understanding of how the site is used.

Your choices

You can ask us to update, correct or remove information you have shared with us where it is reasonable and lawful to do so. Some information may need to be retained where it forms part of legitimate business records.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@pastpaths.co.uk.
