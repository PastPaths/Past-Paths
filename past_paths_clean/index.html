What is Past Paths?

Professional audio experiences for heritage organisations.

Past Paths creates bespoke digital audio experiences for heritage organisations across the United Kingdom.

Each experience is researched and produced for the individual site, allowing visitors to explore at their own pace while helping partners generate additional income.

No upfront cost

We cover production

Past Paths invests in the research, writing and production of each digital experience, removing financial risk for partner sites.

Bespoke content

Made for your site

Every tour is tailored to the history, layout and character of the place it interprets.

Revenue shared

A new income stream

Visitors purchase the tour through the app, and income is shared with the heritage partner.
